Salesforce Import
Two files from Salesforce — drop both and the app joins them automatically by Opportunity Name.
📋 How to export both files from Salesforce
FILE 1 — Opportunities
1.Go to Opportunities tab
2.Select your list view (All Opportunities or North Africa)
3.Click Printable View from the gear ⚙ menu
4.Select all → Copy → Paste into Excel → Save as opportunities.csv
Columns needed: Account Name, Opportunity Name, Domain, Industry, Stage, Probability (%), Opportunity Status, Last Modified Date, Account Territory, Sales Potential
FILE 2 — Products (optional but recommended)
1.Open any Opportunity record
2.Scroll to the Products related list
3.You'll see: Opportunity Name, Product Description, Quantity, List Price, Sales Price, Discount, Total Price
4.Select all rows → Copy → Paste into Excel → Save as products.csv
The app joins on Opportunity Name — multiple products per opp are comma-joined automatically
